Album · 2020 · Recorded in France, mixed in Austria

Take Your Time

This album has been a drawn-out project, to say the very least. It’s called Take Your Time for good reason.

The songs are a selection of those written over my musical lifespan — chosen and gathered together to reflect different times and memories. I write pretty much everything on acoustic guitar and arrangements get built from there on up. Some of these tracks are completely naked, some sit in a traditional band setting, and some carry full orchestral arrangements.

The tracks were recorded in France with a selection of phenomenal musicians and mixed in Austria with my musical mate Mike Blake. I hope you enjoy the snapshots of feelings I have put together — and that, in time, they may become part of the soundtrack to your life.

The story so far

Behind the Music

I’ve been fortunate enough, for the majority of my life, to make a living out of something I love doing — making music.

This life of song has allowed me to travel the world, meet some wonderful people and play with some astounding musicians. When I set out on my very own troubadour’s adventure I had no idea where it would take me. So far, it’s been a hoot…

I never wanted to be a “poor, starving musician” — and thankfully, starve I haven’t. Work has always found me. I have played in different bands, in different line-ups, on different instruments, performing both original and ‘borrowed’ material.

I’ve written with some legendary performers and recorded with some of my childhood heroes. I’m so thankful for all the folk whose paths have crossed, or run parallel to mine, however briefly, over the years.

Thanks for listening — see you somewhere soon…
